Welcome to Cents, a Financial Decision Engine.

Note: This project is currently under development

UI Mockups -V1

The foundation of this idea is to help prepare prospective and current college students for the realities of their educational choices. There are a lot of graduates that have entered the workforce with a large amount of debt and without the prospect of jobs to allow them to pay off that debt.

Our tool would provide information, in a very intuitive way, that would allow a student to see the possible impact of their educational choices before they cost them.

It is our hope to help people make wise decisions. The knowledge we are providing can be found by searching the internet but is usually generalized and non-intuitive. We hope to eliminate this barrier by allowing a user to retrieve information that is both specific to them and also easy to understand.
